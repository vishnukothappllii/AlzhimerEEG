Early Detection of

Project Importance

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive neurologic disorder that causes the brain to shrink (atrophy) and brain cells to die. Early detection is critical for management. This project leverages Electroencephalogram (EEG) signals, which are non-invasive and cost-effective, to detect early patterns of alpha-power changes and coherence abnormalities associated with the disease.
